France’s Got Talent turned steamy in 2014 when Roméo and Lada auditioned for the show. The couple stripped down to their underwear in a sexy burlesque routine.

The performance continues to cause a stir years later, as it has over 21 million views on YouTube. Check it out below, and read on to learn more about this couple.

Couple Performs ‘France’s Got Talent’ Burlesque Routine

Roméo and Lada started the performance in matching tuxedos. Lada’s white shirt was slightly sheer, giving a glimpse of her bra underneath. Other than that, they were pretty covered up.

Then the James Bond theme kicked in, and the pair started to strut around each other. Eventually, they both removed their jackets, then ripped off their shirts. Lada tore off her trousers to reveal her black lingerie.
