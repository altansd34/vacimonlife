10 Pairs of Famous Sisters Whove Become Queens of the Spotlight

August 22, 2024
Celeb

Having a sister can make you happier and more optimistic, according to a study. And as a celebrity, it’s very beneficial to have someone who understands you deeply. There are pairs of sisters who jumped into the spotlight and became famous both as sisters and individually as well. Their talent and support for each other make them very noticeable in the media.

Bright Side presents you with 10 pairs of celebrity sisters who’ve become total queens of the spotlight.

1. Elle and Dakota Fanning

Elle and Dakota both jumped into the spotlight when they were just kids. They were both kid actresses in successful movies and are still conquering Hollywood today.

2. Zooey and Emily Deschanel

These sisters are known for starring in hit shows. Emily is a leading actress in the series, Bones and Zooey one of the main actresses in the hit show, New Girl. Zooey also has a band called She & Him.

3. Noah and Miley Cyrus

Miley and Noah were born into a family of stars. Their father Billy Ray Cyrus was already a successful singer and their brother Tracy Cyrus was part of a band that was quite trendy in its day. Miley became famous after she played the protagonist on the show, Hannah Montana but is most known as a pop singer. Noah followed in Miley’s steps and also became a singer.

4. Solange and Beyoncé Knowles

Solange and Beyoncé both became famous as a result of being amazing performers. Both sisters decided to pursue a singing career and although Beyoncé is more famous, Solange also has a large, loyal fan base.

5. Serena and Venus Williams

These 2 are at the top of the list when it comes to the best tennis players. Although the sisters sometimes compete against each other, there’s no better duo than when they play as a team.

6. Bella and Gigi Hadid

Bella and Gigi followed in their mother’s footsteps and both became supermodels. The sisters are in their twenties and already have been on the covers of famous magazines and walked on many important runways.

7. Ashley and Mary-Kate Olsen

Mary-Kate and Ashley became famous as kids after starring in the sitcom, Full House where they took turns appearing on-screen. They became millionaires very quickly and by the age of 18, they took control of their company Dualstar which they founded when they were just 6 years old. They now own several clothing lines.

9. Nicky and Paris Hilton

These socialites are heiresses of the Hilton Hotels. They first became famous for throwing crazy parties. Paris also had a modeling career but is now a high paid DJ. Nicky has handbag and jewelry lines.

10. Haylie and Hilary Duff

Hilary became famous as a child actress when she starred in the Disney Channel series, Lizzie McGuire. Haylie is also an actress best known for her role in 7th Heaven.
