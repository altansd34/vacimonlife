The promotional video has Hall’s setlist in the form of miss calls. The phone number that shows is a new way that celebrities market themselves to their fans. The list includes Halls hits such as “Rainin Fellas,” “Dripeesha,” and “Boys in the Ocean.” The Femuline tour is based off of Hall’s 2021 album of the same name. Songs from the Celebrity Big Brother alum’s Femuline Gaymeova will also be performed as well throughout the show.

Hall will perform close to 40 songs each night of his tour. With that many songs, his show will last over two hours.
