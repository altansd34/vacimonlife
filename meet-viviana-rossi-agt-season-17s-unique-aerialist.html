Viviana Rossi is auditioning for America’s Got Talent Season 17. Viviana is an aerialist who performs a unique routine involving a bathtub. She has shown off her awe-inspiring talent on other television shows, so fans cannot wait to see her on the AGT stage.

Viviana Rossi Is Competing on ‘AGT’ Season 17

According to Viviana’s Instagram bio, she’s an athlete, model, and aerialist. In the past, she’s worked with Cirque Du Soleil’s stunt division. Viviana is also a gymnastics champion, nutritionist, and trainer. She’s performed aerial routines on television over the years.

In 2020, Viviana competed on Românii Au Talent, which is Romania’s version of the Got Talent franchise. She started her performance in a bathtub full of water, before being lifted up on a strap to perform acrobatic moves in the air.

Viviana was lowered back into the tub and lifted up again, flicking water around her as she moved, holding on with just her hand. She ended the performance by holding on with just her head as she spun around in the air. The water definitely added an unexpected element to the performance that sets Viviana apart.

Viviana Reached the Finals of Another Show

The aerialist also reached the Finals of the Italian talent show Tú Si Que Vales in 2019. For her final performance, she ditched the bathtub and performed some mind-blowing spins and flips while hanging over the stage.

Viviana also performs other aerial talents. For example, she has a video on her YouTube channel in which she performs an aerial pole routine. It will be interesting to see how many different variations on her talent she can showcase on AGT if she gets through her audition.

We can’t wait to see Viviana’s performance during Tuesday night’s new episode of America’s Got Talent, and to find out what the judges thought of her routine. Based on previews, it looks like she’ll be performing her classic bathtub act.

Tune into America’s Got Talent on NBC to see if Vivianna makes past auditions.
