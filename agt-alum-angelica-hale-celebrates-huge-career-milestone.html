America’s Got Talent star Angelica Hale recently celebrated a major YouTube milestone. The 14 year old singer hit three million subscribers nearly five years after appearing on the competition series.

On July 8, Angelica Hale hosted a 51 minute live stream, to celebrate the accomplishment with her fans. Within the stream, Angelica talked to fans, sang, played piano, and popped confetti with her sister, Abby. Alongside the live stream, the singer shared a minute long video featuring glimpses of the singer performing both on stage and in her home.

“Happy three million, YouTube! I love you guys!” she said in the video. “Thank you so much for following me on my journey and have an amazing rest of your day, or night, wherever you guys are.”

Angelica was an immediate stand out performer due to her outstanding rendition of “Rise Up” by Andra Day. The nine year old excelled through the series, placing in second behind Darci Lynne Farmer. About a year after America’s Got Talent, Angelica celebrated her first million subscribers on YouTube. At 11 years old, Angelica re-appeared on America’s Got Talent for AGT: The Champions.

To celebrate this accomplishment alongside Angelica, here’s our favorite cover songs she’s published on YouTube. Congratulations on three million subscribers Angelica!

Angelica Hale’s Best YouTube Cover Videos

5. “We Don’t Talk About Bruno”

What’s better than Angelica Hale? Two Angelica Hales! In this cover video Angelica dueted herself singing the Encanto hit “We Don’t Talk About Bruno.”

4. “Easy on Me”

Earlier this year, Angelica published an amazing cover of “Easy on Me” by Adele. Within the video she showed off her silky smooth vocals along with her piano skills. It’s hard to remember that Angelica is 14 with songs like this.

3. “Warrior”

Angelica truly impresses with her vocals on “Warrior” by Demi Lovato. I love this cover because her emotion and passion is clearly shown throughout the song in both her voice and facial expressions. I would love to hear Angelica sing more Demi Lovato music!

2. “Shallow”

Angelica explores her lower register with this cover of “Shallow” by Lady Gaga. This singer made this song her own and it sounds great because of it. I love seeing her show off her vocal range.

1. “Rise Up”

This is our favorite cover by Angelica, purely for the nostalgia. We loved hearing her sing this song at nine years old and we love it even more now. It’s crazy to see how much her voice as developed since auditioning for AGT with this song in 2017.
