Last night, our man Luke Islam left the show and although his fans were sad to see this future Broadway star go, Julianne Hough was hit the hardest. On AGT semifinals, the 12 year old sang ‘Never Enough,’ from ‘The Greatest Show.’

Here is a video of Julianne Hough expressing her feelings about Luke Islam’s Elimination

After his performance at the AGT semifinals, Julianne had said, “I feel like a proud mama right now. You know what, Luke? You were a voice for all of those people that have felt like they have never been enough.” She added, “thank you for articulating that. You are beautiful. You are more than enough. And I cannot wait to see you in the Finals because you’ve got to vote, vote, vote.”

AGT judge, Gabrielle Union had commented after his performance that “Luke, you know how special I think you are and I think the world agrees with all of us. You are a world class talent. I will say it every time you perform.” She added, echoing our sentiment, “you are a world class talent and you deserve to be in the Finals. Way to go!”

Luke Islam posted on his Instagram, “You guys I cannot say THANK YOU enough for this life changing time with @AGT! Big hugs to the entire #AGT family! @juleshough thank you thank you thank you for all your support and for the #GoldenBuzzer that changed everything and for believing in me (and thank you @brookslaich for being so awesome!) Thank you so much to all the fans – your messages really kept me going thru all of this! Thank you!!!! Love Luke!”

His elimination came as a surprise to most viewers and judges. What do you guys think? Are you as sad as we are to see Luke Islam go? Let us know!
