Gabrielle Union, 51, Says Greasy Skin Helps Her Look Young

August 04, 2024
Celeb

At 51, Gabrielle Union looks just as youthful and radiant as she did almost two decades ago. Many admire and wonder about her beauty secrets that keep her looking ageless. A major part of her timeless look is her naturally greasy skin, which gives her a youthful glow. She stays active and eats healthily to maintain a balanced lifestyle.

Her positive attitude and sincere smile are the real reasons behind her ageless charm. Gabrielle Union serves as an inspiration for many, proving that age is just a number when you prioritize self-care and well-being.

She is grateful for her oily skin.

Many people believe that having oily and greasy skin can be problematic, but research actually suggests that oily skin produces more melanin, which can help protect against the aging effects of sun damage. While this might contribute to anti-aging benefits for oily skin, there’s more to the story.

Oily skin is known to be less prone to developing wrinkles. And Gabrielle Union openly acknowledges having naturally oily skin throughout her life. As she’s grown older, she’s come to appreciate it, as this natural oil acts somewhat like embalming fluid, helping to keep her skin looking youthful.

She drinks a lot of water.

Water is crucial for keeping your body refreshed and your skin elastic. Those who drink lots of water are less likely to get scars, wrinkles, and lines, and they look younger than those who don’t drink much water.

The actress reveals that she strives to drink a gallon of water daily, and this has had a remarkable impact on her hair, skin, and nails. Thanks to her high level of hydration, she rarely experiences major skin issues. She considers this to be her best and simplest beauty secret.

She always protects her skin from the sun.

Some people wrongly think that those with dark skin are safe from sun damage and don’t need sunscreen. But this is a mistake.

Gabrielle Union highlights that many people with dark skin believe their natural melanin provides all the protection they need. However, this isn’t true. Even people with dark skin aren’t immune to the potential harm from sun exposure, and sun protection is crucial for everyone, regardless of their ethnicity.

She combines different skincare products.

The main advantage of blending skincare products is the ability to tailor your skincare routine to match your specific skin needs more effectively. This is especially helpful if you have multiple skin concerns to address, while also considering your skin type. Whether you’re aiming to restore balance, enhance your skin’s radiance, combat pigmentation, or reverse the signs of aging, creating a personalized skincare mix can help you reach your individual goals.

Gabrielle Union keeps all her cleansers in her shower, and her choice depends on how she feels that day. If she wants an extra kick, she opts for the oil-free scrub because it’s quite potent. If she wakes up with a noticeable blemish on her face, she tackles it with an acne-specific cleanser.

She keeps her eyebrows natural.

The actress suggests that not plucking your eyebrows can make you look younger. When asked about her go-to beauty look, she mentioned it’s as simple as using lip balm and keeping your eyebrows full but well-groomed. She believes that thicker eyebrows are a great way to combat the signs of aging. Over-plucking can result in overly sharp and unflattering brows, so trying to look like Cruella de Vil should not be your beauty goal.

Gabrielle Union is a strong supporter of embracing natural beauty, even when it comes to her daughter’s hair. When people began commenting on her daughter’s hair not always being styled, Gabrielle responded with confidence, celebrating her daughter’s natural curly hair. She thinks it’s crucial to teach children to love and embrace themselves as they are, including their natural hair.
