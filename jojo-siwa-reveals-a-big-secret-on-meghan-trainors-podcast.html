JoJo Siwa is Planning to Disguise Herself

While guest appearing on Meghan Trainor’s podcast JoJo Siwa, their topic of conversation led to Siwa to bring up her girl group. Siwa said that group is her finest work, and they she’s proud that they’re having multiple shows on a cruise.

Siwa then opened up her playful side and basically told the hosts of the podcast about a big secret she’s pressing. She revealed that she wanted to go on disguise in one of the cruise shows, to either give a happy surprise to XOMG POP! or to prank them for a video.

“Okay tell me to tell you something when we’re off of this because I have a secret that I would love, love, freakin love for you to be a part of,” Siwa said. “We should go on a cruise just for funsies, and we’re gonna go find a disguise.”

For those wondering, Siwa didn’t reveal the reasoning behind the plan to disguise on one of her girl group’s shows. Surprisingly enough, Siwa even wants to add Trainor to make the secret mission a memorable one. Siwa is Training For Something Right Now

Another thing you would notice on Siwa during her guest appearance is that her right arm is lightly wrapped with KT Tape. She revealed that she’s undergoing a heavy workout and diet routine right now, and has gotten to the best shape of her life.

“I’m physically training for a lot of things right now, so gymnastics training and working out like insane.” Siwa said. “I can’t say what for yet but, you’ll find found eventually.”
