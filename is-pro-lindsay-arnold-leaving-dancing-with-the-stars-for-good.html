Is Dancing with the Stars pro dancer Lindsay Arnold leaving the show for good? The star, who is currently pregnant with her second child, took the most recent season off. Her future on the show is up in the air, especially after a recent post.

Is Lindsay Arnold Leaving Dancing with the Stars?

Recently, Arnold posted an article in her Instagram stories from Good Morning America. The article’s title refers to the dancer as a “former Dancing with the Stars pro.” Heavy suggests this could be a clue that Arnold has left the show behind.

The outlet points out that Good Morning America is “an authority” on the show, having covered it since it first premiered in 2005. Is this recent wording evidence that Arnold is done with DWTS, or is it simply referring to the fact that she hasn’t appeared on the show since Season 30?

In October of last year, Arnold spoke about the possibility of returning to the show, telling E! News, “I’ll never say never to Dancing with the Stars, it is part of who I am.” She added, “That door will never be closed.”

Recently, Arnold has been focused on her company The Movement Club, in which she leads workouts. The dancer has been posting about the venture on Instagram.

Arnold Is Expecting Her Second Child

Last year, Arnold shared that she would not be competing on DWTS Season 31, as she didn’t want to make the move from her home in Utah to Los Angeles for the show. The dancer, who shares a daughter named Sage with her husband Sam Cusick, also said they were trying to have another baby.

It wasn’t long before Arnold announced that she was pregnant with her second child, another girl. Recently, Arnold caused fans to speculate that she was expecting twins after they saw that she wrote the phrase “unborn daughters” on a social media post.

Arnold later clarified in Instagram stories that it was just a typo, and she’s only expecting one baby.
