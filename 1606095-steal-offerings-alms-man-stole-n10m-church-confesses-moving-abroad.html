"Was an usher in a big church in Lagos. Me nd my friends we usually steal offering, alms, tithe, seeds, etc."Stole up to 10m from church. I don stop. Thank God. Married now with children, living well abroad."Most ushers in big churches are tiff. One offering alone fit reach 7m. I know so many in Lagos."
