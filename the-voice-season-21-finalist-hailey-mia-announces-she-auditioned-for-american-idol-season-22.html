'The Voice' Season 21 Finalist Hailey Mia Announces She Auditioned For 'American Idol' Season 22

Hailey Mia, a talented young singer who was a finalist on The Voice Season 21, has recently announced that she auditioned for American Idol Season 22. The teen singer announced the news in a recent Instagram post, where she shared an official trailer for the new season.

Hailey Mia Auditioned on American Idol

In a trailer video for the upcoming season of American Idol, Mia can be seen briefly singing in one clip, sparking excitement among her fans. The show is set to premiere in February 2024 on ABC, and her fans are eagerly anticipating her appearance in the popular singing competition.

Mia captured the hearts of audiences with her powerful performances on The Voice Season 21, despite facing challenges due to a rare medical condition. Although Mia was the youngest competitor her The Voice season, she was still able to make it to the top, finishing in fourth place.

Mia’s decision to audition for American Idol Season 22 has generated significant buzz and anticipation within the music industry and among her dedicated fan base. Her unique vocal abilities and stage presence are reasons to make her a strong contender in the competition.

As for American Idol Season 22, the show is celebrating its 20th anniversary with judges auditions in various locations across the United States. Expect the upcoming season to blend former contestants and new talent, emphasizing original songs and showcasing diverse musical styles.

Hailey Mia Might Be The Youngest Contestant on American Idol Season 22

At just 16 years old, Mia is certainly making waves as a standout contestant on the latest season of American Idol. Born on September 1, 2007, this talented young artist hails from Clifton, New Jersey, and is also captivating audiences with her exceptional musical abilities.

At the age of 8, Mia’s journey into the spotlight began when she underwent surgery to address deteriorating vision in her right eye. The procedure, detailed in her NBC biography, successfully enabled Mia to regain over 30 percent of her vision. Despite the obstacles, she embarked on a remarkable journey of self-discovery, teaching herself how to read music and play the piano.

Despite her youth, Mia’s passion for music has been evident for years. In 2020, she shared a video of herself performing “Bruises” by Lewis Capaldi outside the Willowbrook Mall in Wayne, New Jersey, showcasing her raw talent and dedication to her craft.
