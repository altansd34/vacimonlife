Couples with an age gap bigger than 10 years tend to experience social disapproval, claim various studies. But that doesn’t seem to stop those in the spotlight to invest their time and affection in partners who are either much older or much younger than them. In fact, many beloved famous couples who have been married for years have big age gaps. We gathered here a few of those successful love stories.

Matthew McConaughey and Camila Alves, 13-year age gap

The couple met at a club in 2006, and as the actor said, “We had a great connection. I spoke better Spanish and Portuguese that night than I ever had.” Today, the couple has 3 children and a strong relationship. “We have a relationship that we never question,” said McConaughey.

Alicia Vikander and Michael Fassbender, 11-year age gap

Michael Fassbender says that he fell in love with Alicia Vikander in 2014 while filming the movie, The Light Between Oceans. They played a couple in love in the film, and soon their on-screen romance became a reality. In 2021, the 33-year-old actress and the 44-year-old actor had their first child.

Paige Butcher and Eddie Murphy, 18-year age gap

Since 2012, Eddie Murphy has been in a relationship with model Paige Butcher. The actor is 18 years older than his partner, and the couple has 2 children.

Brittany Lopez and Christian Slater, 18-year age gap

Brittany and Christian met in 2009. The actor was 39 then, and the girl he liked was 21. Soon they got married and became parents 10 years later.

Jeff Goldblum and Emilie Livingston, 30-year age gap

Josh and Kathryn Brolin, 20-year age gap

Kathryn was Josh Brolin’s personal assistant. In 2013, they started dating and got married in 2016. Now the couple has 2 children.

Rosalind Ross and Mel Gibson, 34-year age gap
