Dancing with the Stars co-host Julianne Hough will be joining the show’s live tour next year. Dates for 2024 were just announced, with tickets going on sale this Friday. Hough confirmed her involvement during Tuesday’s new episode of the show.

Julianne Hough Joins 2024 DWTS Live Tour

The DWTS live tour is a tradition after each season of the show, as the pro dancers hit the road to perform for fans around the country. Celebrity contestants also make appearances at the shows. This year, former pro and current co-host Julianne Hough will be in the show.

During Tuesday’s new episode of DWTS, Hough announced the 2024 version of the tour, sharing that pros Brandon Armstrong, Britt Stewart, Alan Bersten, Daniella Karagach, Pasha Pashkov, Gleb Savchenko, and Rylee Arnold will be appearing. Emma Slater will once again be the host.

Hough also announced her own involvement in the new tour, saying, “I am so excited, because I am actually gonna be joining these pros to dance in select cities.”

In Season 4, Hough joined DWTS as a pro dancer. She won the Mirrorball in her first two seasons, with Apolo Anton Ohno and Hélio Castroneves. Hough continued as a pro until Season 8. She later returned as a judge for several seasons before becoming a co-host alongside Alfonso Ribeiro for Season 32.

Tickets Go On Sale This Friday

The 2024 DWTS live tour kicks off January 11 in Richmond, Virginia. The show has dates lined up around the United States and Canada into March. According to the website, Hough will appear in eight cities, including the final show in Los Angeles.

Tickets for the tour officially go on sale this Friday, October 20. However, fans can sign up to be a Mirrorball Member to participate in a pre-sale and receive updates about the tour. You can sign up for free here.
