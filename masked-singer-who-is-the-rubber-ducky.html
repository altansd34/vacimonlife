Season 10 of The Masked Singer started with an amazing kickoff and first reveal when Demi Lovato was unmasked. So now, they have a LOT to top the rest of the season! One of the singers taking the stage looking to bring the fun and vocals is Rubber Ducky.

Will Rubber Ducky have a chance at the Golden Mask though? And who is the celebrity under the mask? Here’s everything we know so far!

Clues on The Masked Singer Season 10

The Rubber Ducky’s clues were a mixed bag! We saw a VHS that said “The Horror! 3-4” on it. We also saw a framed picture of Leonardo DiCaprio. The Rubber Ducky mentioned that he’s tried stand-up comedy. He claimed that his family is “full of stars.”

Popular Guesses for The Masked Singer Rubber Ducky

The panel of judges predicted Rubber Ducky could be David Arquette, Jaleel White, or Anthony Anderson. Popular guesses online included Marlon Wayans, Keegan Michael Key, Anthony Anderson, and Pete Davidson. Really at this point, it’s anyone’s guess!

Who is the Rubber Ducky on The Masked Singer?

After his first performance, the Rubber Ducky was revealed to be…

Anthony Anderson!

But there are plenty more unmaskings where that came from. The Masked Singer is back Wednesdays at 8/7c on FOX. And you won’t want to miss season 10!
