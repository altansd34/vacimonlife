Celebrities often capture our attention with their glamorous lifestyles and high-profile relationships. However, every now and then, they surprise us with their relatability and down-to-earth nature. Selena Gomez recently caught the internet’s attention with a playful TikTok video, where she expressed her single status to a group of soccer players during a game.

Selena Gomez Shouts Her Single Status to Soccer Players

On June 8, Gomez took to TikTok to share a lighthearted moment from her life. In the video, she is seen seated on the sidelines of a soccer field, surrounded by the energy of the game. Seizing the opportunity, Gomez shouted to the players in the midst of their match, revealing her relationship status with a resounding, “I’m single!”

Although most of the players were likely too far away to hear her, Gomez’s fans, always quick to engage, rallied behind her in the comments section. Many applauded her for being open and relatable, urging her to continue expressing herself authentically.

While Gomez did not reveal the duration of her singlehood, it is not uncommon for public figures to experience ups and downs in their relationships. Over the years, Gomez has been linked to notable figures like Justin Bieber and Nick Jonas. Most recent of all, rumors swirled about her and former One Direction member Zayn Malik.

However, her recent TikTok video reminded fans that she is embracing her independence and approaching life with a sense of humor and authenticity. By doing so and expressing herself with confidence, Gomez reminded her audience that it’s okay to be unapologetically single and empowered.
