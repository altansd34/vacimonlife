America’s Got Talent contortionist Sofie Dossi recently paid a visit to the show’s set and had some fun with the judges. She also challenged host Terry Crews to a contest of strength.

Dossi competed on the show in Season 5, making it to the Top 10. She later returned for AGT: The Champions in 2019. She’s known for her dramatic contortion performances, often involving a bow and arrow.

Sofie Dossi Returns to ‘AGT,’ Challenges Terry Crews

Dossi posted a video about her return to the show on YouTube. She shared that it had been five years since she first auditioned for the show at age 14. She was excited to reunite with the judges, as her brother Zak joked that she was returning to the show as a comedian and personal trainer.

We got to see Dossi try out her new talents for the judges and host. First, she tried out her comedy material for judge Howie Mandel. She even delivered a joke while bent into a contortion pose.

Dossi then moved on to Crews, whom she described as “skinny and weak.” She displayed a handstand/split combo and challenged Crews to try it himself. He gave it his best effort, with a little assistance from Dossi. Next was a backbend, which Crews also struggled with.

They ended the workout routine with some weight lifting, and the results might surprise you. As Dossi suggested, “Maybe I’m just stronger than you.”

Heidi Klum Gives Dossi Dating Advice

Dossi finished her visit to the AGT set by chatting with judge Heidi Klum about boys. Dossi said she was having trouble narrowing down her options when it comes to dating. Klum suggested that if none of them stood out to Dossi as being special, she shouldn’t pick any of them.

Mandel soon joined the conversation, offering a hypothetical situation in which a guy has great-smelling breath but terrible body odor. The discussion then takes an even stranger turn, if you can believe it.

It has us excited to reunite with the AGT judges for Season 16, premiering Tuesday at 8 p.m. ET on NBC.
