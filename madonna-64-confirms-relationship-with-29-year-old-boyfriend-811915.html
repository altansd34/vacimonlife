Madonna, 64, Confirms Relationship With 29-Year-Old Boyfriend

August 08, 2024
Celeb

A study suggests that women who date younger men are happier and more satisfied in their relationships, and the queen of pop is here to prove it. Putting the rumors to rest, Madonna shared a sweet photo of her boo and herself sharing a kiss.

It’s never too late to find love.

Last week, the internet speculated if Madonna had a new man in her life when boxing coach Josh Popper shared a series of photos in which the singer can be seen lovingly wrapped around his arm. Josh is 29 years old, and he currently appears in the reality TV show Summer House. He’s also allegedly training one of Madonna’s kids.

The couple is going strong.

Confirming the rumors, the 64-year-old diva has taken to her Instagram story and shared a photo of the couple kissing. The pair is dressed in all black, wearing the same colored masks, and attending a party together. She was also spotted cheering for her boyfriend during one of his matches.

Madonna broke up with her ex last month.

Madonna dated model Andrew Darnell, who 41 years younger than her, for 5 months. “It was only a very casual thing...so she’s not broken-hearted,” a source close to her revealed. Previously, she dated dancer Ahlamalik Williams for 3 years, breaking up in April of last year. The couple had a 35-year age gap.

She maintains her youthful appearance and doesn’t care what people think.

The singer admitted to getting another facial surgery but then called out the ageism in society when her Grammy look made headlines for the wrong reasons.

“Once again I am caught in the glare of ageism and misogyny that permeates the world we live in. A world that refuses to celebrate women past the age of 45,” she said about the situation, adding that she has never apologized for her choices and never will. She also called her new look “cute.”
