19 Intimate Movie Moments That Show What Films Look Like Before Editing

August 14, 2024
Celeb

We tend to judge films by their plot and neglect other aspects. Cinematography, direction, special effects, and editing, among other things, are fundamental to creating a complete film experience. As the behind-the-scenes images and stories often do not transcend, we do not learn about the work that goes on behind the scenes. To reevaluate our vision of movies, we prepared the following compilation of pics that show just how hard everyone works in a film crew.

1. Eugenio Derbez receiving instructions from director Richard Wong while filming The Valet (2022).

2. This scene from Don’t Worry, Darling (2022) is also one of the advertising posters. Here we see Florence Pugh’s character after giving a kiss to Harry Styles’ character.

3. Picture taken during the filming of director Paco de León’s Rainbow (2022). He can be seen in the first image’s background.

4. Tom Hardy and Venom figure face to face during filming and in Venom: Carnage (2021).

5. The Wasteland’s production (2022) holding sheets while filming a scene against the wind.

6. Actress and dinosaur dance to TikTok choreography in The Bubble (2022).

7. Zoe Saldaña preparing to play Gamora in Guardians of the Galaxy Vol. III.

8. Behind the scenes of Mad God (2021), an adult animated film shot in a stop-motion style. It took 30 years to produce.

9. Production used a scale model to portray the large crowd shots in Star Wars: The Phantom Menace (1999).

10. Mark Ruffalo preparing to play the Hulk.

11. Picture taken while filming a scene in Black Bear (2022), an action movie from one of the genre’s kings, Liam Neeson.

12. How the spaceship scene was filmed for Apollo 10 1/2: A Space Age Adventure (2022).

13. Producer Jon Landau shared a series of photos showing how actress Sigourney Weaver (Kiri in the film) shot certain action scenes from the Avatar sequels.

14. Part of the main cast of Fantastic Beasts: The Secrets of Dumbledore (2022) listening attentively to the film’s director, David Yates.

15. How the Dog (2022) production crew filmed the scenes in the car.

16. Behind the scenes of Uncharted (2022) with Tom Holland taking cues from its director.

17. Tom Cruise laughing with the producers and director of the movie Top Gun: Maverick (2022).

18. In the first photo, director Matt Reeves gives directions to Robert Pattinson during the filming of Batman (2022).
