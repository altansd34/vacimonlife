14 Ways in Which Shrek Inspired Game of Thrones

August 10, 2024
Celeb

Shrek was released in 2001. The film tells the adventures of a green and grumpy ogre and his friend, a donkey, as they try to rescue an enchanted princess. We can find similarities between the characters or the plot of this film with many other productions, but, undoubtedly, the ones it has with the acclaimed series Game of Thrones are numerous.

We at Bright Side were impressed with the resemblance we could find between the two and have created this list for you to see them too.

1. The costumes of Jaime Lannister and Prince Charming.

2. Similarities in filmmaking

3. Do not forget the presence of dragons in both stories.

4. Dothraki look like villagers.

5. In both productions there are masked people.

6. Podrick looks like the human version of Shrek.

7. Both Arya and Puss in Boots know how to handle a sword.

8. Olenna Tyrell has as many tricks up her sleeve as the Fairy Godmother.

9. Both stories show two short men with a huge personality.

10. Like Jon Snow, King Harold’s true identity is revealed sometime later in the story.

11. Seeing Hodor carrying Bran is like seeing Mongo carrying Shrek.

12. The characters look alike even when they bring out their romantic side.
