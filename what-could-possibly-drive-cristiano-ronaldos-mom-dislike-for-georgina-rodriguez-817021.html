What Could Possibly Drive Cristiano Ronaldos Mom Dislike for Georgina Rodriguez

August 22, 2024
Celeb

Cristiano Ronaldo is known as one of the best soccer players ever, and he’s also a family man with five kids. He lives with his girlfriend, Georgina Rodriguez and his mother Dolores Aveiro, and it turns out, the relationship between these two women is not always perfect.

Why Ronaldo prefers to live with his mother.

AFP/EAST NEWS, AFP/EAST NEWS

Cristiano Ronaldo deeply admires and loves his mother, Maria Dolores dos Santos Aveiro. He experienced firsthand the sacrifices and dedication she made while raising him, and he always remembers the importance of their relationship.

Ronaldo shared more about his decision, saying that his mother raised him by sacrificing her life for him. “She went to bed hungry to feed me. We had no money. She worked 7 days a week and worked nights as a cleaner to buy me a pair of [soccer] boots, so I could play.”

Ronaldo holds his mother in high regard, giving credit to her support and sacrifices for his success. When asked about their relationship, he mentioned that all his life’s achievements are dedicated to her. He plans to keep providing her with everything he can.

Dolores and Georgina don’t get along well.

Pacific Press/Sipa USA/East News

Recently, it came out that Ronaldo’s mom and his girlfriend don’t get along well. This was talked about on a Portuguese TV show called Em Família. A guest, António Bravo, discussed the strained relationship between Aveiro and Rodriguez.

Bravo suggested that this might be a recurring issue for Ronaldo’s mom with his girlfriends. He thinks she should ease up a bit to let her son be happy.

It could be a quarrel because of a family painting.

Marechal Aurore/ABACA/Abaca/East News, Amr Nabil/Associated Press/East News

There were rumors that Ronaldo’s mom played a part in his breakup with his ex-girlfriend, Irina Shayk. It was said that Shayk didn’t attend Aveiro’s 60th birthday, and this led to their breakup.

A Portuguese journalist, Adriano Silva Martins, shared some details about why there’s tension between Ronaldo’s mom and his girlfriend. It started when Rodriguez moved a cherished painting from their home during renovations. Ronaldo’s mom was upset about it, and now there seems to be ongoing tension between them.

Martins mentioned that there’s no healthy relationship between them, and they seem to dislike each other.

Dolores could feel that Georgina wanted to replace her.

CRISTINA QUICLER/AFP/East News

In the cozy living room, right above the fireplace, there was once a large painting showcasing Cristiano Ronaldo alongside his beloved mother. It served as a beautiful symbol of their close bond and shared moments. However, a noticeable change occurred when Georgina Rodriguez, Ronaldo’s girlfriend, moved into the household.

To Dolores Aveiro’s surprise, she discovered that the special painting featuring her and Cristiano had been taken down and relocated to the garage. In its stead, Georgina had positioned a new painting—one of herself.

For Dolores, the replacement of the original painting with Georgina’s portrait felt like more than just a decorative switch. It struck a chord with her, leaving her with the impression that Georgina might be trying to assert herself or even replace her in the family dynamic. The symbolism behind the change seemed to convey a shift in the household dynamics, with Dolores feeling a sense of perceived superiority on Georgina’s part.

Every family encounters its own set of challenges and problems. It’s a natural aspect of familial dynamics. However, what distinguishes healthy families is their willingness to address and discuss these issues openly. Communication plays a vital role in resolving conflicts and strengthening relationships within a family.
